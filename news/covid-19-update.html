COVID-19 Update

  • Innovative Home
  • News & Events

As COVID-19 continues to have global impact, we remain focused on protecting the health of our employees and communities. We have always put our employee safety first, and that commitment has never been more important than it is today.

Although Thailand has been largely successful in stemming the tide of infections, Innovative Gloves is seeing unprecedented requests for emergency assistance from other major parts of the world this year.

Unfortunately, in the race against this pandemic, we are also seeing an increase in fraudulent and criminal activity, having recently been informed that a number of our own customers have received emails from agents purporting to sell some of our products.

In seeking to maintain the integrity of our gloves, we urge that all distributors remain vigilant and perform their due diligence when purchasing from any “agents” claiming to represent the Innovative Gloves brand. Please remain especially cautious of those promising short lead times and/or unusually low prices. If in doubt, please contact us at sales@innovativeglove.com

Additionally, since our ability to ship out orders promptly is less certain than normal in view of supply-side disruption, we would also like to urge customers to anticipate requirements as far in advance as possible to better facilitate on-time delivery.

Finally, to all the safety & healthcare heroes who have been working around the clock for the past several months, our gratitude for you is endless. On behalf of the entire Innovative Gloves family, thank you all over and over.

I wish all our customers and their families good health during this unusual and challenging time. We are all in this together and will get through this together.

Take care and stay safe,
Rajeev Sood, President & CEO
Innovative Gloves
