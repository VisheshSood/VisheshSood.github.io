During the oil crisis of 2007, industries across the globe, including glove manufacturing, faced significant challenges as oil prices surged by over 300% in a matter of days. This rapid increase in costs imposed a heavy financial burden on companies, many of which could not pass these rising costs onto customers and were overwhelmed by mounting debt.

At **Innovative Gloves**, our proactive and strategic approach enabled us to quickly adapt to this crisis. We successfully applied for a **clean coal burning permit** from the government, which was initially cautious about issuing such permits due to concerns over environmental impact. Many factories had historically misused these permits, burning low-quality coal that contributed to significant pollution and environmental damage.

Leveraging our strong reputation within the industry and community, the government approved our application. We committed to sourcing only the **highest-quality coal** imported from Indonesia, ensuring minimal pollutant emissions and adhering to the highest environmental standards.

Currently, we operate **three clean coal-burning boilers/heaters**, which supply ample heat to support **14 production lines**. Our most recent boiler was custom-developed in-house to meet our specific operational needs, incorporating **state-of-the-art technology** that minimizes environmental impact. Through ongoing **research and development**, we have significantly increased the efficiency of the burning process, reducing coal consumption while maintaining optimal production output.

At **Innovative Gloves**, we believe that a commitment to both quality and environmental responsibility is essential. Our clean coal technology is just one example of how we continue to improve the efficiency of our operations while contributing to a healthier planet.
