Generally speaking, the safety needs in the food processing industry can be categorized into two types: low care and high care. Beyond this, every user will have their own preference between latex and nitrile gloves based on their own desired level of comfort. Regardless of whichever food processing application you are dealing with, we’re confident that you’ll find a glove well-suited to your needs at Innovative Gloves.

Innovative Gloves is one of the only manufacturers in the world to carry out off-line chlorination, a process by which a glove’s strength and comfort is significantly enhanced. This is a highly labour-intensive, and not to mention time- and resource- consuming process. However, at Innovative Gloves we are committed to producing only the best quality disposable gloves and understand what goes into delivering that promise, which is why all our latex gloves undergo this treatment and have near-neglible extractable protein content, making them the perfect glove for food processing.

The diamond texture on our gloves is an added bonus for grip and safety.
