There is little argument that, for decades now, the automotive market has been deprived of quality disposable gloves. For many years, engineers and technicians have been supplied disposable gloves designed primarily for dentists and nurses, with very little consideration given to their unique industry-specific needs.

However, since the introduction of synthetic rubber (otherwise known as nitrile), Innovative Gloves has disrupted the disposable glove manufacturing industry by taking this previously untapped user need, and transforming it into chemical formulations and textures which enable tougher and more reliable disposable gloves that specially cater to the needs of end-users in the automotive industry.

Innovative Gloves was one of the first manufacturers to develop a patented nitrile formulation that promises all end-users in the automotive industry with disposable gloves that not only ensure top tensile strength and elongation, but also provide unrivaled grip in both wet and dry conditions.

We also believe that the quality of industrial disposable gloves should, in every way possible, match those sold to medical professionals.
