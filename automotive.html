Automotive Industry

  • Innovative Home
  • Industries
  • Automotive Industry

The automotive market has been neglected of good gloves for decades. For several years, engineers and technicians were given the same gloves given to dentists and nurses, without considering the difference in the industries. However, ever since synthetic rubber (nitrile) was introduced, Innovative Gloves has led the manufacturing industry to produce unique and special products catering to the automotive industry through special formulations and patented designs to help all those in the automotive industry enjoy what they do on a daily basis.

Innovative was one of the first manufacturers to develop a nitrile formulation that guarantees unbelievable tensile strength and elongation, and with their unique patented designs, we also provide the best grip in the industry at a very affordable price.

We also believe that the quality of industrial gloves should match that of medical industries. The last thing a mechanic would want is a torn glove and oily fingers. Thus, our gloves go through 12 stages of quality control before they reach you, ensuring you will never don a torn glove.
