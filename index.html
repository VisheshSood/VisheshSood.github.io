World's Leading Glove Manufacturer

In the competitive landscape of the disposable gloves industry, Innovative Gloves has emerged as a distinguished and reputable manufacturer, dedicated to crafting gloves of the highest quality for end-users. Despite facing challenging economic conditions and fierce competition, our unwavering commitment has propelled us to prominence in Thailand.

Our exceptional reputation is exemplified by our impeccable track record, with not a single container being rejected by buyers to date. This testament to our unwavering dedication speaks volumes about the trust and satisfaction our gloves provide.

Currently, IGCL proudly produces over 1 billion gloves annually, with expansion efforts underway. We have successfully established the necessary infrastructures, including the erection of boilers, and we continue to invest in training programs to ensure our team is equipped to manage our growth. At IGCL, we firmly believe in balanced expansion across all segments of our company, encompassing production, quality assurance, administration, and comprehensive training initiatives.

Our consistent focus on innovation and the integration of cutting-edge technologies has not only ensured our survival in this dynamic industry but has also captured the attention of niche segments of buyers seeking unique offerings to introduce to the market.

As we forge ahead, driven by our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Innovative Gloves remains at the forefront of quality manufacturing in Thailand, catering to the evolving needs of the disposable gloves market.
