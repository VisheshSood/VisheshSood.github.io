Awards

Corporate Social Responsibility : Department Of Industrial Works

In 2015, Innovative Gloves was the only Glove manufacturing company to be awarded with the CSR-DIW Award, along with only 120 other companies across Thailand. This shows how much Innovative Gloves cares about their employees, neighbours and surroundings and proves that social responsibility is an integral part of its policy.

Innovative Gloves has proven that we provide gloves that are safe, effective and meet the quality standards set by Canada's Medical Devices Regulations (MDR) Department.
